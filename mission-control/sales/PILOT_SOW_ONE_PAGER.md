# Pilot SOW One-Pager (AVO / AccessFlow)

## Pilot Name
SCIF Schedule-Risk Sprint (2 Weeks)

## Objective
Reduce immediate schedule risk by removing the top accreditation/access sequencing bottlenecks affecting active secure projects.

## Scope (Included)
1. **Rapid Diagnostic (Days 1-2)**
   - Stakeholder intake (PM/security/facility)
   - Current-state bottleneck map
2. **Execution Plan (Days 3-7)**
   - Prioritized remediation actions
   - Owner-by-owner sequencing plan
   - Escalation workflow for blockers
3. **Handoff Package (Days 8-14)**
   - Readiness summary
   - Action tracker + timeline view
   - Leadership brief (risks removed + open items)

## Deliverables
- SCIF schedule-risk map (v1)
- 14-day action board with owners
- Escalation protocol
- End-of-sprint executive summary

## Client Responsibilities
- Single point of contact
- Timely access to project artifacts/inputs
- Decision availability for blocker resolution

## Out of Scope (Pilot)
- Full accreditation authority representation
- Legal/compliance certification services
- Long-term PMO replacement

## Timeline
- 10 business days (2 weeks)

## Commercials
- Fixed-fee pilot (see pricing options)
- 50% kickoff / 50% upon sprint closeout

## Success Criteria
- Top 3 bottlenecks identified and assigned
- Action plan in execution with named owners
- Measurable reduction in schedule uncertainty
