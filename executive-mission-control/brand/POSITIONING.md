# Citadel Glass — Positioning

## Brand Positioning Statement
Citadel Glass provides discreet, high-trust residential glass protection for executives and high-profile households that need privacy, resilience, and white-glove execution.

## Messaging Pillars
1. **Discretion First** — low-visibility process, professional protocol
2. **Protection That Performs** — practical hardening outcomes, not hype
3. **White-Glove Execution** — concierge scheduling, clean install, precise communication

## Tagline Options
- Quiet Strength for Critical Homes
- Discreet Protection. Exceptional Delivery.
- Executive Home Security, Refined
- Private by Design. Strong by Default.
