# Citadel Glass — Brand Guide (Web)

## 1) Brand Essence

**Positioning:** Discreet, high-trust residential glass protection for executive and high-profile households.  
**Brand Promise:** Premium protection planning and white-glove delivery without spectacle.  
**Core Idea:** Private by design. Strong by default.

---

## 2) Voice & Tone

### Voice Attributes
- **Discreet:** Never loud, never alarmist
- **Authoritative:** Calm expertise, operational precision
- **Refined:** Executive-grade language, minimal fluff
- **Reassuring:** Confidence without over-promising
- **Service-Led:** Concierge orientation, clarity at every step

### Tone by Context
- **Hero/Top of Page:** Elevated, composed, high-trust
- **Service Descriptions:** Specific, factual, outcome-oriented
- **FAQs/Compliance:** Clear, transparent, legally safe
- **CTAs:** Confident and direct, never pushy

### Language Guidelines
**Use:**
- “residential hardening”
- “privacy and glass-performance enhancement”
- “discreet protocol”
- “white-glove execution”
- “executive fit call”

**Avoid:**
- Fear-based phrasing (“under attack,” “imminent threat”)
- Absolute guarantees (“bulletproof,” “unbreakable,” “100% secure”)
- Hype words (“ultimate,” “revolutionary,” “game-changing”)

---

## 3) Messaging Pillars

1. **Discretion First**  
   Low-visibility engagement model, controlled communication, professional protocol.

2. **Protection That Performs**  
   Practical, standards-based improvements to privacy and glass retention characteristics.

3. **White-Glove Execution**  
   Concierge planning, clean install quality, structured handoff.

---

## 4) Typography Suggestions

### Primary (Headlines)
- **Canela** (premium editorial serif)  
  Fallback: `"Iowan Old Style", "Times New Roman", serif`

### Secondary (Body/UI)
- **Inter** (high legibility, modern professional sans)  
  Fallback: `-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, sans-serif`

### Type Scale (Suggested)
- H1: 56/60, medium tracking
- H2: 40/46
- H3: 30/36
- Body Large: 20/32
- Body: 17/28
- Small/Meta: 14/22

### Weight Usage
- Headlines: 500–600
- Body: 400–450
- Labels/Meta: 500

---

## 5) Color Palette (Ultra-Premium, Low-Noise)

### Core Colors
- **Onyx Black** — `#0E1116` (primary background / dark sections)
- **Graphite** — `#1A212B` (surfaces, cards, nav)
- **Stone Gray** — `#6E7782` (secondary text)
- **Ivory Mist** — `#F5F3EE` (primary light background)
- **Pure White** — `#FFFFFF` (high-contrast content)

### Accent Colors
- **Citadel Gold** — `#B79A63` (premium accent, rules, CTA highlights)
- **Steel Blue** — `#3B556E` (trust accent for data/protocol UI)

### Functional
- Success: `#3F6B57`
- Warning: `#8A6A3B`
- Error: `#8A3F3F`

### Usage Ratios
- 70% neutrals (Onyx/Graphite/Ivory)
- 20% text and structure tones
- 10% accent (Gold/Steel) for focus and hierarchy

---

## 6) Visual Direction

- Minimal, architectural layouts with generous whitespace
- Tight content hierarchy and restrained iconography
- Photography style: premium residential exteriors/interiors, low-saturation, natural shadows
- Avoid tactical/militarized imagery and “fear marketing” visuals

---

## 7) CTA & Conversion Style

### CTA Principles
- Lead with clarity, not urgency pressure
- Keep friction low (“20-minute fit call”)
- Always offer a secondary low-commitment path (one-page overview)

### Recommended CTA Labels
- **Book a 20-Minute Executive Fit Call**
- **Request the Obsidian One-Page Overview**
- **Speak with a Program Advisor**

---

## 8) Compliance & Claim Safety

### Required Claim Posture
- Describe solutions as risk-reduction and performance-enhancement measures
- Clarify that no single measure eliminates risk
- Avoid absolute outcomes or guarantees

### Suggested Standard Disclaimer
“Citadel Glass solutions are designed to improve privacy and glass performance characteristics. Outcomes vary by existing glass/frame conditions, installation context, and event-specific factors. No film solution guarantees prevention of forced entry or eliminates all security risk.”

---

- Discreet Protection. Exceptional Delivery.
- Executive Home Security, Refined.
- Private by Design. Strong by Default.
