Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this designed for?

Citadel Glass is purpose-built for executive households, family offices, and principals who require discreet residential hardening with high service standards.

Do your films make glass “unbreakable”?

No. Security films are designed to improve glass retention and delay breach progression in many scenarios, but they do not make glass indestructible or guarantee specific outcomes.

Can you coordinate with estate managers or security teams?

Yes. We regularly coordinate with executive assistants, chiefs of staff, estate managers, and security stakeholders under defined protocol.

Important Performance Disclaimer

Citadel Glass solutions are intended to improve privacy, reduce visibility, and strengthen glass performance characteristics. No film or installation method can guarantee prevention of forced entry, eliminate all risk, or replace comprehensive physical security planning. Performance depends on glass type, frame condition, installation quality, and event-specific factors.
